About the Project

The objective of this project was to produce an interactive film in which the user's choices determine which path they will follow in the story. It allows the users to feel like their decisions matter, while also encouraging them to explore the other path of the story and become immersed in the planning process between the characters.

The story begins with Isabella and Navya discussing their plans for the fall break, and ultimately deciding they will travel to another country. While one character expresses her desire of travelling to Paris, the other wants to explore Dubai, two countries that were carefully chosen for the contrast in their environmental aspect.
